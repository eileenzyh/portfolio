UNDECIMBER


Clothing and graphic designs depicting the year of 2020.

Position: Co-founder, Art director. 2020-2021


As waves of COVID-19 permeates the world, forest fires sweeps Australia, and the BLM movement commences ... 2020 was a year that left a permanent mark in our lives, rerouting our paths. Among a time of uncertainty, we as global citizens and witnesses would like to collect voices around the world, and display these stories of 2020 through our unique perspective. We collected 60+ stories from people around the world. After interviewing and surveying, we decided to use clothing as a way of representing these voices.


Why clothing? We all have to wear something. As we wear these designs, we're presenting and spreading these ideas in a subtle but effective way.




PART I - 6 Keywords & Designs


We extracted 6 keywords that appeared multiple times from the stories collected, and used these keywords as inspiration for designs.
